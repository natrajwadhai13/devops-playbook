# **📘 Common English Words for Daily & Professional Communication**

## 📘 Important English Words – Hindi / Marathi Meanings

| English Word   | Hindi Meaning       | Marathi Meaning     | English Use / Meaning         | Example Sentence                  |
| -------------- | ------------------- | ------------------- | ----------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| **Can**        | सकता हूँ / सकती हूँ | शकतो / शकते         | Ability or permission         | I can complete this task.         |
| **Could**      | सकता था / सकती थी   | शकत होतो / शकत होते | Past ability / polite request | Could you help me?                |
| **What**       | क्या                | काय                 | Asking about a thing          | What is the issue?                |
| **Where**      | कहाँ                | कुठे                | Asking about place            | Where is the server located?      |
| **When**       | कब                  | केव्हा              | Asking about time             | When will the meeting start?      |
| **Why**        | क्यों               | का                  | Asking reason                 | Why is the job failing?           |
| **How**        | कैसे                | कसे                 | Asking method                 | How did you fix it?               |
| **Fond of**    | शौकीन होना          | आवड असणे            | Like something a lot          | I am fond of automation testing.  |
| **Had better** | बेहतर होगा          | जास्त चांगलं राहील  | Strong advice                 | You had better update the system. |
| **Need**       | ज़रूरत होना         | गरज असणे            | Requirement                   | I need access to the repo.        |
| **Want**       | चाहना               | इच्छा असणे          | Desire                        | I want to learn DevOps.           |
| **Have to**    | करना पड़ता है       | करावं लागतं         | Compulsion                    | I have to finish this today.      |
| **Should**     | चाहिए               | पाहिजे              | Advice                        | You should practice daily.        |
| **Must**       | ज़रूरी है           | आवश्यक आहे          | Strong obligation             | You must follow the process.      |
| **May**        | सकता है             | शक्य आहे            | Possibility / permission      | You may join the call.            |
| **Might**      | हो सकता है          | कदाचित              | Weak possibility              | It might rain today.              |
| **Able to**    | सक्षम होना          | सक्षम असणे          | Capability                    | I am able to solve this issue.    |
| **Agree**      | सहमत होना           | सहमत असणे           | Accept opinion                | I agree with your point.          |
| **Depend on**  | निर्भर करना         | अवलंबून असणे        | Based on something            | It depends on the requirement.    |
| **Instead of** | की जगह              | ऐवजी                | Replacement                   | Use Git instead of FTP.           |

---

### 🔹 How to use this for learning

1. **Read meanings** (Hindi + Marathi)
2. **Speak example sentence loudly**
3. Try to **create 1 new sentence daily**
