# Articles (A, An, The)

## What is an Article?

Articles are words used before nouns to indicate whether the noun is general or specific.

Articles are:

* A
* An
* The

**Hindi:** Article ऐसे शब्द हैं जो Noun के पहले लगाए जाते हैं।

**Marathi:** Noun च्या आधी वापरले जाणारे शब्द म्हणजे Articles.

---

# Types of Articles

There are two types of Articles:

## 1. Indefinite Articles

* A
* An

Used for general or non-specific nouns.

### Examples

* A boy
* A car
* An apple
* An engineer

---

## 2. Definite Article

* The

Used for a specific noun.

### Examples

* The sun
* The moon
* The manager
* The server

---

# Article "A"

## Rule

Use **A** before a word that begins with a consonant sound.

### Examples

| Correct  | Meaning    |
| -------- | ---------- |
| A boy    | एक मुलगा   |
| A car    | एक कार     |
| A laptop | एक लॅपटॉप  |
| A server | एक सर्व्हर |

### Sentences

* I bought a laptop.
* She drives a car.
* We deployed a server.

---

# Article "An"

## Rule

Use **An** before a word that begins with a vowel sound.

Vowels:

```text
A, E, I, O, U
```

### Examples

| Correct     | Meaning    |
| ----------- | ---------- |
| An apple    | एक सफरचंद  |
| An orange   | एक संत्रे  |
| An engineer | एक अभियंता |
| An umbrella | एक छत्री   |

### Sentences

* I ate an apple.
* He is an engineer.
* She bought an umbrella.

---

# A vs An

## Easy Rule

### Consonant Sound → A

* A book
* A dog
* A teacher
* A server

### Vowel Sound → An

* An apple
* An engineer
* An orange
* An hour

---

# Special Cases

English me sound important hoti hai, spelling nahi.

## Use "An"

### An hour

❌ A hour

✅ An hour

Reason:

Hour me "h" silent hai.

Sound = "our"

---

### An honest person

❌ A honest person

✅ An honest person

"H" silent hai.

---

## Use "A"

### A university

❌ An university

✅ A university

Reason:

University ka sound "yu" se start hota hai.

---

### A European country

❌ An European country

✅ A European country

Sound = "yu"

---

# Article "The"

## Rule

Use "The" when talking about a specific person, place, thing, or object.

### Examples

* The sun
* The moon
* The Earth
* The manager
* The server

---

## Specific Noun

### Example

I saw a dog.

(Dog kaunsi? Pata nahi)

I saw the dog.

(Ab specific dog ki baat ho rahi hai)

---

## DevOps Examples

* The Docker container stopped.

# When NOT to Use Articles

### General Statements

❌ The milk is good for health.

✅ Milk is good for health.

---

❌ The honesty is important.

✅ Honesty is important.

---

### Languages

❌ The English is easy.

✅ English is easy.

---

### Subjects

❌ The Mathematics is difficult.

✅ Mathematics is difficult.

# Key Points

✅ A = Consonant Sound

✅ An = Vowel Sound

✅ The = Specific Person, Place, or Thing

✅ Sound is more important than spelling

✅ Always use Articles in professional English

✅ Articles are very important in Emails, Interviews, Meetings, and Daily Conversations
