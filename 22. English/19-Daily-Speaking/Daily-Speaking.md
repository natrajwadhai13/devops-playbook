# Daily Speaking English

## Introduction

This chapter is designed for daily English speaking practice.

The goal is not to speak perfect English.

The goal is:

✅ Speak every day

✅ Think in English

✅ Build confidence

✅ Improve fluency

---

# 12. Daily Speaking Patterns

## Pattern 2


# Key Points

✅ Speaking improves by speaking, not by reading only.

✅ Make small sentences first.

✅ Speak daily for at least 15–30 minutes.

✅ Don't fear mistakes.

✅ Think in English.

✅ Use office and DevOps examples.

✅ Record your voice and listen.

✅ Consistency is more important than perfection.
