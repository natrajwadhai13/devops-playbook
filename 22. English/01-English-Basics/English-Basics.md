# English Basics

## What is English Grammar?

English Grammar is a set of rules that helps us speak, write, and understand English correctly.

**Hindi:** English Grammar vah niyam (rules) hai jo hame English sahi tarike se bolne aur likhne me madad karta hai.

**Marathi:** English Grammar म्हणजे इंग्रजी भाषा योग्य पद्धतीने बोलण्यासाठी आणि लिहिण्यासाठी लागणारे नियम.

### Example

❌ I goes to office.

✅ I go to the office.

Grammar helps us create correct sentences.

---

# Sentence Structure

A sentence is a group of words that expresses a complete thought.

The most common English sentence structure is:

```text
Subject + Verb + Object
```

### Example

| Subject | Verb  | Object  |
| ------- | ----- | ------- |
| I       | play  | cricket |
| She     | reads | books   |
| We      | watch | movies  |
| They    | eat   | food    |

### More Examples

- I drink tea.
- She writes emails.
- We learn English.
- They play football.

---

# Subject, Verb, Object (SVO)

## Subject

The person, animal, place, or thing that performs the action.

### Examples

- I play cricket.
- Natraj works in an IT company.
- The dog barks.

In these sentences:

- I → Subject
- Natraj → Subject
- Dog → Subject

---

## Verb

A verb shows an action or state.

### Examples

- run
- eat
- write
- work
- play
- speak

### Sentences

- I work in an office.
- She writes emails.
- They play cricket.

---

## Object

The object receives the action.

### Examples

| Subject | Verb  | Object  |
| ------- | ----- | ------- |
| I       | eat   | rice    |
| She     | reads | a book  |
| We      | watch | a movie |

---

## Complete SVO Examples

### Example 1

I eat rice.

| Part    | Word |
| ------- | ---- |
| Subject | I    |
| Verb    | eat  |
| Object  | rice |

### Example 2

She reads a book.

| Part    | Word   |
| ------- | ------ |
| Subject | She    |
| Verb    | reads  |
| Object  | a book |

### Example 3

Natraj writes emails.

| Part    | Word   |
| ------- | ------ |
| Subject | Natraj |
| Verb    | writes |
| Object  | emails |

# Types of Sentences

There are four main types of sentences.

---

## 1. Declarative Sentence (Statement)

Used to give information.

### Examples

- I am a DevOps Engineer.
- She lives in Nagpur.
- We are learning English.

---

## 2. Interrogative Sentence (Question)

Used to ask questions.

### Examples

- What is your name?
- Where do you work?
- Are you learning English?

---

## 3. Imperative Sentence (Command / Request)

Used to give instructions, advice, or requests.

### Examples

- Open the door.
- Please help me.
- Learn English daily.

---

## 4. Exclamatory Sentence (Strong Feeling)

Used to express emotions.

### Examples

- What a beautiful day!
- Amazing!
- Congratulations!
