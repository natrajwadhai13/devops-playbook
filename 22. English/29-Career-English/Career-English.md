# Career English

## Introduction

Career English is the English required for:

- Job Search
- Resume Writing
- LinkedIn Profile
- Interviews
- Meetings
- Professional Communication
- Career Growth

This chapter focuses on English that helps you grow professionally.

---

## Hindi Meaning

# Talking About Experience

## Formula

```text
I have + Experience + in + Skill
```

---

## Examples

- I have 7 years of experience in Linux Administration.
- I have experience in AWS and Docker.
- I have worked on production support projects.

---

# Talking About Skills

## Examples

```text
My technical skills include:

- Linux
- AWS
- Docker
- Kubernetes
- Jenkins
- Terraform
- Git
```

---

## Professional Sentence

```text
I have hands-on experience with Linux, Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, AWS, and Terraform.
```

---

# Talking About Responsibilities

## Examples

- I manage Linux servers.
- I support deployment activities.
- I monitor application performance.
- I troubleshoot production issues.
- I automate infrastructure tasks.

# Powerful Resume Verbs

Avoid weak words.

---

## Weak

- Did
- Made
- Worked
- Helped

---

## Strong

- Managed
- Implemented
- Automated
- Configured
- Optimized
- Designed
- Integrated
- Maintained
- Monitored
- Resolved

# Resume Achievement Writing

## Formula

```text
Action + Result
```

---

## Examples

### Basic

❌ Managed servers.

### Better

✅ Managed 100+ Linux servers and ensured 99.9% availability.

---

### Basic

❌ Worked on deployments.

### Better

✅ Automated deployment processes using Jenkins and reduced manual effort.

# Talking About Projects

## Formula

```text
Project

Objective

Technology

Result
```

---

## Example

```text
I worked on an infrastructure automation project.

The objective was to automate server provisioning.

We used Terraform and AWS.

The solution reduced deployment time significantly.
```

# Key Points

✅ Career English is professional English.

✅ Focus on achievements, not tasks.

✅ Use strong action verbs.

✅ Highlight results and impact.

✅ Communicate confidently.

✅ Build a strong LinkedIn and GitHub profile.

✅ Practice self-introduction regularly.

✅ Career growth depends on both technical and communication skills.

✅ Strong English + Strong DevOps Skills = Better Opportunities.

### Your Professional Introduction (Final Version)

```text
My name is Natraj Wadhai.

I am a DevOps Engineer with 7 years of experience in Linux administration, cloud technologies, deployment management, and automation.

I have experience working with AWS, Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, Terraform, and Git.

Currently, I am working at Infosys and supporting infrastructure and deployment activities.

I am continuously improving my technical and communication skills and looking for opportunities to grow as a Senior DevOps Engineer.
```

Ye introduction aap LinkedIn, interviews, meetings aur professional networking sab jagah use kar sakte ho.
