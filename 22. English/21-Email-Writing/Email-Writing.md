# Email Writing

## Introduction

Email Writing is one of the most important skills in professional communication.

A good email should be:

- Clear
- Professional
- Polite
- Short
- Easy to Understand

---

## Hindi Meaning

Email Writing का मतलब है पेशेवर तरीके से ईमेल लिखना।

---

## Marathi Meaning

Email Writing म्हणजे व्यावसायिक पद्धतीने ईमेल लिहिणे.

---

# Structure of a Professional Email

A professional email has 5 parts:

```text
1. Subject

2. Greeting

3. Email Body

4. Closing Line

5. Signature
```

---

# Email Format

```text
Subject: Email Subject

Hello Team,

Email Content

Thanks,
Natraj
```

---

# 1. Subject Line

The subject should clearly explain the purpose.

---

## Good Subject Examples

- Deployment Completed Successfully
- Production Issue Update
- Leave Request for 25 June
- Server Maintenance Notification
- Request for Access Approval

---

## Bad Subject Examples

❌ Hi

❌ Important

❌ Please Check

---

# 2. Greeting

Use professional greetings.

---

## Team Email

- Hello Team,
- Good Morning Team,
- Hi Team,

---

## Individual Email

- Hello John,
- Hi David,
- Dear Manager,

---

# 3. Email Body

The body should explain:

- Why you are writing
- Current status
- Required action

---

# 4. Closing

Common closings:

- Thanks,
- Regards,
- Best Regards,
- Kind Regards,

---

# 5. Signature

```text
Thanks,
Natraj Wadhai
DevOps Engineer
```

---

## Example

I will share the final update shortly.

Thanks,
Natraj

## Example

Thanks,
Natraj

Natraj

# Professional Email Phrases

## Starting

- I would like to inform you that...
- Please note that...
- This email is regarding...
- I would like to request...

---

## Requesting

- Could you please...
- Kindly review...
- Please approve...
- Please verify...

---

## Updating

- The activity is in progress.
- The task has been completed.
- The issue has been resolved.
- Validation is ongoing.

---

## Closing

- Please let me know if you have any questions.
- Kindly confirm.
- Thank you for your support.

---

# Common Email Mistakes

## Wrong

```text
Hi

Issue fixed

Thanks
```

---

## Correct

```text
Hello Team,

The issue has been resolved successfully.

Thanks,
Natraj
```

---

## Wrong

```text
Deployment done
```

---

## Correct

```text
The deployment has been completed successfully.
```

---

## Wrong

```text
Check and tell.
```

---

## Correct

```text
Kindly verify and confirm.
```

# Key Points

✅ Always write a clear Subject.

✅ Start with a Greeting.

✅ Keep emails short and professional.

✅ Use complete sentences.

✅ Avoid chat language in emails.

✅ Use professional words:

- Resolve
- Verify
- Confirm
- Inform
- Request

✅ Always end politely.

✅ Email Writing is one of the most important career skills.

✅ Good emails create a professional image.
