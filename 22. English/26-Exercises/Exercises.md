# Key Points

✅ Practice is more important than theory.

✅ Speak every day.

✅ Write something every day.

✅ Use English in office chats.

✅ Record your voice weekly.

✅ Learn from mistakes.

✅ English is a skill, not a subject.

✅ Small daily practice creates big results.

### Personal Exercise for You

Every day answer these 5 questions in English:

```text
1. What did I do yesterday?

2. What am I doing today?

3. What did I learn today?

4. What issue did I solve?

5. What will I do tomorrow?
```

Agar aap roz sirf in 5 questions ka answer English me likhoge, to 3–6 months me speaking aur writing dono me bahut improvement aayega.
