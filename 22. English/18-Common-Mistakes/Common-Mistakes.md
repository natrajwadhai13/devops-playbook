# Common English Mistakes

## Introduction

This chapter covers the most common mistakes made by Marathi and Hindi speakers while speaking and writing English.

Learning these mistakes is one of the fastest ways to improve your English.

---

## Hindi Meaning

यह अध्याय उन सामान्य गलतियों के बारे में है जो हम अंग्रेज़ी बोलते और लिखते समय करते हैं।

---

## Marathi Meaning

हा अध्याय इंग्रजी बोलताना आणि लिहिताना होणाऱ्या सामान्य चुका समजावून सांगतो.

# Mistake 1: I Am vs I

### Wrong

❌ I working on the issue.

### Correct

✅ I am working on the issue.

---

### Wrong

❌ I learning English.

### Correct

✅ I am learning English.

---

## Rule

Whenever you use:

```text id="m1a8v4"
Verb + ing
```

You usually need:

```text id="x5c7t2"
am / is / are
```

---

# Mistake 2: He/She + Verb

### Wrong

❌ He work in Infosys.

### Correct

✅ He works in Infosys.

---

### Wrong

❌ She play cricket.

### Correct

✅ She plays cricket.

---

## Rule

For:

- He
- She
- It

Add:

```text id="r8y4n6"
s / es
```

to the verb in Simple Present Tense.

---

# Mistake 3: Use of Articles

### Wrong

❌ I am engineer.

### Correct

✅ I am an engineer.

---

### Wrong

❌ I bought laptop.

### Correct

✅ I bought a laptop.

---

## Rule

Singular countable nouns usually need:

- A
- An
- The

---

# Mistake 4: Much vs Many

### Wrong

❌ Many information.

### Correct

✅ Much information.

---

### Wrong

❌ Much servers.

### Correct

✅ Many servers.

---

## Rule

| Word | Use         |
| ---- | ----------- |
| Many | Countable   |
| Much | Uncountable |

---

# Mistake 5: Few vs Little

### Wrong

❌ Few time.

### Correct

✅ Little time.

---

### Wrong

❌ Little servers.

### Correct

✅ Few servers.

---

## Rule

| Word   | Use         |
| ------ | ----------- |
| Few    | Countable   |
| Little | Uncountable |

---

# Mistake 6: Since vs For

### Wrong

❌ I have worked here since 2 years.

### Correct

✅ I have worked here for 2 years.

---

### Wrong

❌ I have worked here for 2024.

### Correct

✅ I have worked here since 2024.

---

## Rule

### Since

Starting Point

- Since Monday
- Since 2024

### For

Duration

- For 2 years
- For 5 days

---

# Mistake 7: In, On, At

### Wrong

❌ In Monday

### Correct

✅ On Monday

---

### Wrong

❌ At July

### Correct

✅ In July

---

### Wrong

❌ On 10 PM

### Correct

✅ At 10 PM

---

## Easy Rule

```text id="a3w7k8"
In → Month / Year

On → Day / Date

At → Exact Time
```

---

# Mistake 8: Do / Does

### Wrong

❌ Does he works here?

### Correct

✅ Does he work here?

---

### Wrong

❌ Do you knows him?

### Correct

✅ Do you know him?

---

## Rule

After:

- Do
- Does
- Did

Use:

```text id="q2m5r1"
V1
```

---

# Mistake 9: Did + V1

### Wrong

❌ Did you completed the task?

### Correct

✅ Did you complete the task?

---

### Wrong

❌ Did he went home?

### Correct

✅ Did he go home?

---

## Rule

After Did:

Use V1 only.

---

# Mistake 10: Have vs Has

### Wrong

❌ He have completed the work.

### Correct

✅ He has completed the work.

---

### Wrong

❌ We has completed testing.

### Correct

✅ We have completed testing.

---

## Rule

### Has

- He
- She
- It

### Have

- I
- You
- We
- They

---

# Mistake 11: Subject-Verb Agreement

### Wrong

❌ The servers is running.

### Correct

✅ The servers are running.

---

### Wrong

❌ The engineer have a laptop.

### Correct

✅ The engineer has a laptop.

---

## Rule

Singular → is / has

Plural → are / have

---

# Mistake 12: Can / Could / Should / Must

### Wrong

❌ He can works.

### Correct

✅ He can work.

---

### Wrong

❌ We should to deploy.

### Correct

✅ We should deploy.

---

### Wrong

❌ We must to complete.

### Correct

✅ We must complete.

---

## Rule

After Modal Verbs:

```text id="p6n3x5"
Use V1
```

---

# Mistake 15: Direct and Indirect Speech

### Wrong

❌ He said me.

### Correct

✅ He told me.

---

### Wrong

❌ She told that she was busy.

### Correct

✅ She said that she was busy.

---

# Mistake 16: Capitalization

### Wrong

❌ i work in infosys.

### Correct

✅ I work in Infosys.

---

### Wrong

❌ i am from nagpur.

### Correct

✅ I am from Nagpur.

---

## Rule

Always capitalize:

- I
- Names
- Cities
- Companies
- Months
- Days

---

# Mistake 17: Apostrophe

### Wrong

❌ Its working.

### Correct

✅ It's working.

---

### Difference

Its = Possession

It's = It is

---

### Examples

- The server lost its configuration.
- It's running properly.

---

# Mistake 18: Double Negatives

### Wrong

❌ I don't know nothing.

### Correct

✅ I don't know anything.

---

### Wrong

❌ I can't find nobody.

### Correct

✅ I can't find anybody.

---

# Mistake 19: Wrong Word Choice

### Basic

❌ I did deployment.

### Better

✅ I deployed the application.

---

### Basic

❌ I fixed issue.

### Better

✅ I resolved the issue.

---

### Basic

❌ I did monitoring.

### Better

✅ I monitored the infrastructure.
