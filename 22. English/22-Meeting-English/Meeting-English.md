# Meeting English

## Introduction

Meetings are an important part of office communication.

Good Meeting English helps you:

✅ Speak confidently

✅ Share your ideas clearly

✅ Ask questions professionally

✅ Participate actively

✅ Improve communication skills

---

# Daily Standup Meeting

Most IT companies follow 3 questions.

---

## Yesterday

What did you do yesterday?

### Example

* Yesterday, I worked on deployment activities.
* Yesterday, I monitored production servers.
* Yesterday, I resolved two incidents.

---

## Today

What will you do today?

### Example

* Today, I will work on automation tasks.
* Today, I will investigate the issue.
* Today, I will monitor deployments.

---

## Blockers

Do you have any blockers?

### Example

* I do not have any blockers.
* I am waiting for access approval.
* I need support from the database team.

---

# Common English Mistakes

## Wrong

❌ I not understand.

### Correct

✅ I do not understand.

---

## Wrong

❌ Repeat again.

### Correct

✅ Could you repeat that?

---

## Wrong

❌ What you mean?

### Correct

✅ What do you mean?

---

## Wrong

❌ Explain again.

### Correct

✅ Could you explain that again?

# Key Points

✅ Speak clearly and slowly.

✅ Use polite questions.

✅ Participate actively.

✅ Share updates confidently.

✅ Ask for clarification when needed.

✅ Don't be afraid of mistakes.

✅ Most meetings use simple English.

✅ Good Meeting English improves confidence and career growth.

✅ Focus on:

* Updates
* Questions
* Clarifications
* Status Reporting

✅ Practice daily standup updates every day.
