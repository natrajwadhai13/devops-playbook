# Office Examples

## Introduction

This chapter contains real-world office communication examples.

These examples are commonly used in:

- Teams Chat
- Emails
- Meetings
- Daily Standups
- Client Calls
- Incident Calls
- DevOps Operations

# Key Points

✅ Use complete sentences.

✅ Always provide status updates.

✅ Keep communication professional.

✅ Use polite language.

✅ Communicate clearly and concisely.

✅ Most office communication uses simple English.

✅ These examples can be used directly in Teams chats, meetings, and emails.

✅ Practice these examples daily until they become natural.



### Special Practice for You

Every day in Teams, avoid:

❌ Checking

❌ Done

❌ Looking

Use:

✅ I am checking.

✅ The task has been completed.

✅ I am looking into the issue.

Ye small changes aapki office English ko immediately professional bana denge.
