# Office English

## Introduction

Office English is the English used in:

- Office Communication
- Teams Chat
- Emails
- Meetings
- Status Updates
- Client Discussions

This chapter focuses on practical office communication.

---

# Common English Mistakes

## Wrong

❌ I am checking and update you.

### Correct

✅ I am checking and will update you.

## Wrong

❌ Issue fixed.

### Correct

✅ The issue has been fixed.

---

## Wrong

❌ Deployment completed.

### Correct

✅ The deployment has been completed.

## Wrong

❌ Looking.
