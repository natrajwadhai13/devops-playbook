# Helping Verbs (Auxiliary Verbs)

## What are Helping Verbs?

Helping Verbs are verbs that help the main verb in a sentence.

They do not usually show the main action themselves. They help show:

- Time (Tense)
- Possibility
- Ability
- Negation
- Questions

---

## Hindi Meaning

Helping Verbs वे क्रियाएँ हैं जो मुख्य क्रिया (Main Verb) की सहायता करती हैं।

---

## Marathi Meaning

Helping Verbs म्हणजे मुख्य क्रियेला मदत करणारी क्रियापदे.

---

# Why are Helping Verbs Important?

Without helping verbs, many English sentences cannot be formed correctly.

### Examples

❌ I working.

✅ I am working.

---

❌ He completed?

✅ Has he completed?

---

❌ We not ready.

✅ We are not ready.

---

# 1. Be Verbs

Be Verbs are:

```text
Am
Is
Are
Was
Were
Being
Been
```

Used for:

- Present Continuous
- Past Continuous
- Passive Voice
- Descriptions

---

# AM

Used with:

```text
I
```

### Formula

```text
I + am
```

### Examples

- I am a DevOps Engineer.
- I am learning English.
- I am working on the issue.

---

# IS

Used with:

```text
He
She
It
Singular Nouns
```

### Examples

- He is working.
- She is studying.
- The server is down.
- The application is running.

---

# ARE

Used with:

```text
You
We
They
Plural Nouns
```

### Examples

- We are learning English.
- They are engineers.
- The servers are healthy.

---

# Common Mistakes

❌ He are working.

✅ He is working.

---

❌ Servers is healthy.

✅ Servers are healthy.

---

# WAS

Used with singular subjects in the past.

### Examples

- I was working.
- He was sleeping.
- The server was down.

---

# WERE

Used with plural subjects in the past.

### Examples

- We were working.
- They were waiting.
- The servers were unavailable.

---

# 3. Do Verbs

Do Verbs are:

```text
Do
Does
Did
```

Used for:

- Questions
- Negatives
- Emphasis

---

# DO

Used with:

```text
I
You
We
They
```

### Examples

- Do you work here?
- Do they know the issue?

---

# DOES

Used with:

```text
He
She
It
Singular Nouns
```

### Examples

- Does he work remotely?
- Does she speak English?
- Does the server respond?

---

# DID

Used with all subjects in the past.

### Examples

- Did you complete the task?
- Did they deploy the application?

---

# Negative Sentences

### Present

```text
Do/Does + not + V1
```

Examples:

- I do not know.
- He does not work on weekends.

---

### Past

```text
Did + not + V1
```

Examples:

- I did not attend the meeting.
- We did not deploy yesterday.

---

# Common Mistakes

❌ Does he works here?

✅ Does he work here?

---

❌ Did you completed it?

✅ Did you complete it?

# Helping Verbs in Tenses

## Present Continuous

```text
am/is/are + Ving
```

Examples:

- I am studying.
- He is working.

---

## Past Continuous

```text
was/were + Ving
```

Examples:

- I was studying.
- They were working.

---

## Present Perfect

```text
has/have + V3
```

Examples:

- I have completed the task.
- He has finished the work.

---

## Past Perfect

```text
had + V3
```

Examples:

- We had deployed the application.
- She had sent the email.

---

# Key Points

✅ Helping Verbs help the Main Verb.

✅ Am/Is/Are → Present

✅ Was/Were → Past

✅ Have/Has/Had → Perfect Tenses

✅ Do/Does/Did → Questions & Negatives

✅ Helping Verbs are the backbone of English Grammar.

✅ Master Helping Verbs before Modal Verbs, Passive Voice, and Advanced Grammar.

✅ 90% of daily office communication uses Helping Verbs.
