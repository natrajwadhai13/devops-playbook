# Parts of Speech

## What are Parts of Speech?

Parts of Speech are categories of words based on their function in a sentence.

**Hindi:** Parts of Speech शब्दों के प्रकार हैं जो बताते हैं कि कोई शब्द वाक्य में क्या काम कर रहा है।

**Marathi:** Parts of Speech म्हणजे वाक्यातील शब्दांचे प्रकार. प्रत्येक शब्द वाक्यात वेगवेगळे काम करतो.

### Example

```text
Natraj writes emails quickly.
```

| Word    | Part of Speech |
| ------- | -------------- |
| Natraj  | Noun           |
| writes  | Verb           |
| emails  | Noun           |
| quickly | Adverb         |

---

# 1. Noun (नाम)

## Definition

A Noun is the name of a person, place, animal, thing, or idea.

**Hindi:** किसी व्यक्ति, स्थान, वस्तु, जानवर या विचार के नाम को Noun कहते हैं।

**Marathi:** व्यक्ती, ठिकाण, प्राणी, वस्तू किंवा कल्पना यांच्या नावाला Noun म्हणतात.

## Examples

### Person

* Natraj
* Ram
* Sita

### Place

* Nagpur
* Mumbai
* India

### Thing

* Book
* Laptop
* Mobile

### Animal

* Dog
* Cat
* Cow

## Office Examples

* Server
* Application
* Database
* Jenkins
* Docker

## Sentences

* Natraj works in an IT company.
* Mumbai is a big city.
* The server is down.

## Common Mistake

❌ I am working on server.

✅ I am working on the server.

---

# 2. Pronoun (सर्वनाम)

## Definition

A Pronoun replaces a Noun.

**Hindi:** जो शब्द Noun की जगह उपयोग होता है उसे Pronoun कहते हैं।

**Marathi:** Noun च्या जागी वापरल्या जाणाऱ्या शब्दाला Pronoun म्हणतात.

## Examples

| Noun            | Pronoun |
| --------------- | ------- |
| Natraj          | He      |
| Sita            | She     |
| Dog             | It      |
| Natraj and Sita | They    |

## Sentences

* Natraj is a DevOps Engineer. He works in Infosys.
* Sita is a teacher. She teaches English.

## Office Examples

* I am checking the issue.
* We completed the deployment.
* They fixed the bug.

## Common Mistake

❌ Natraj is working. Natraj will update.

✅ Natraj is working. He will update.

---

# 3. Verb (क्रियापद)

## Definition

A Verb shows an action or state.

**Hindi:** जो शब्द कार्य या स्थिति बताता है उसे Verb कहते हैं।

**Marathi:** कृती किंवा अवस्था दर्शवणाऱ्या शब्दाला Verb म्हणतात.

## Examples

### Action Verbs

* Run
* Eat
* Write
* Read
* Work

### State Verbs

* Am
* Is
* Are
* Was
* Were

## Sentences

* I work in an office.
* She writes emails.
* They play cricket.

## DevOps Examples

* The server runs successfully.
* Jenkins builds the project.
* Docker deploys the application.

## Common Mistake

❌ He work in Infosys.

✅ He works in Infosys.

---

# 4. Adjective (विशेषण)

## Definition

An Adjective describes a Noun or Pronoun.

**Hindi:** जो शब्द Noun या Pronoun की विशेषता बताता है उसे Adjective कहते हैं।

**Marathi:** Noun किंवा Pronoun ची माहिती किंवा गुणधर्म सांगणाऱ्या शब्दाला Adjective म्हणतात.

## Examples

* Big house
* Beautiful girl
* Fast server
* Good engineer

## Sentences

* She is intelligent.
* It is a large building.
* This is a powerful server.

## DevOps Examples

* Fast deployment
* Secure application
* Stable environment

## Common Mistake

❌ Server is fastly.

✅ Server is fast.

---

# 5. Adverb (क्रियाविशेषण)

## Definition

An Adverb describes a Verb, Adjective, or another Adverb.

**Hindi:** जो शब्द Verb, Adjective या दूसरे Adverb की विशेषता बताता है उसे Adverb कहते हैं।

**Marathi:** Verb, Adjective किंवा दुसऱ्या Adverb बद्दल अधिक माहिती देणाऱ्या शब्दाला Adverb म्हणतात.

## Examples

* Quickly
* Slowly
* Carefully
* Successfully

## Sentences

* He runs quickly.
* She speaks clearly.
* They completed the task successfully.

## DevOps Examples

* The deployment completed successfully.
* The application is running smoothly.
* The issue was resolved quickly.

## Common Mistake

❌ He runs quick.

✅ He runs quickly.

---

# 6. Preposition (संबंध दर्शाने वाले शब्द)

## Definition

A Preposition shows the relationship between words.

**Hindi:** जो शब्द दो चीजों के बीच संबंध बताता है उसे Preposition कहते हैं।

**Marathi:** दोन शब्दांमधील संबंध दाखवणाऱ्या शब्दाला Preposition म्हणतात.

## Common Prepositions

* In
* On
* At
* Under
* Behind
* Between
* Near

## Examples

### In

* In July
* In India
* In the room

### On

* On Sunday
* On the table
* On 15 August

### At

* At 7 PM
* At home
* At the office

## Office Examples

* The meeting is at 10 AM.
* The file is on the server.
* The logs are in the folder.

## Common Mistake

❌ I will come in Monday.

✅ I will come on Monday.

---

# 7. Conjunction (संयोजक)

## Definition

A Conjunction joins words, phrases, or sentences.

**Hindi:** दो शब्दों या वाक्यों को जोड़ने वाले शब्द को Conjunction कहते हैं।

**Marathi:** दोन शब्द किंवा वाक्य जोडणाऱ्या शब्दाला Conjunction म्हणतात.

## Common Conjunctions

* And
* But
* Or
* Because
* Although

## Examples

* I work and study.
* He is smart but lazy.
* You can take tea or coffee.

## Office Examples

* The deployment is completed and verified.
* The server is running but the application is down.
* We delayed the release because testing was incomplete.

---

# 8. Interjection (भाववाचक शब्द)

## Definition

An Interjection expresses emotions or feelings.

**Hindi:** जो शब्द अचानक भावना व्यक्त करता है उसे Interjection कहते हैं।

**Marathi:** अचानक भावना व्यक्त करणाऱ्या शब्दाला Interjection म्हणतात.

## Examples

* Wow!
* Oh!
* Great!
* Congratulations!
* Amazing!

## Sentences

* Wow! The application is working.
* Great! We fixed the issue.
* Congratulations! You got the job.

# Practice Exercise

Identify the Parts of Speech:

1. I work in Infosys.
2. The server is running smoothly.
3. She completed the deployment successfully.
4. Wow! The application is working.
5. We use Docker and Kubernetes.

---

# Answers

1. I (Pronoun), work (Verb), Infosys (Noun)
2. server (Noun), running (Verb), smoothly (Adverb)
3. She (Pronoun), completed (Verb), deployment (Noun), successfully (Adverb)
4. Wow! (Interjection), application (Noun), working (Verb)
5. We (Pronoun), use (Verb), Docker (Noun), and (Conjunction), Kubernetes (Noun)

# Key Points

✅ Every English sentence is made using Parts of Speech.

✅ Noun = Name

✅ Pronoun = Replaces a Noun

✅ Verb = Action

✅ Adjective = Describes a Noun

✅ Adverb = Describes a Verb

✅ Preposition = Shows Relationship

✅ Conjunction = Joins Words

✅ Interjection = Shows Emotion

✅ Learn Parts of Speech first to understand Grammar, Tenses, Speaking, Email Writing, and Office Communication.
