# Modal Verbs

## What are Modal Verbs?

Modal Verbs are helping verbs that express:

- Ability
- Permission
- Possibility
- Advice
- Necessity
- Obligation
- Request
- Suggestion

---

## Hindi Meaning

Modal Verbs ऐसे Helping Verbs हैं जो क्षमता, अनुमति, सलाह, संभावना या आवश्यकता को व्यक्त करते हैं।

---

## Marathi Meaning

Modal Verbs म्हणजे क्षमता, परवानगी, सल्ला, शक्यता किंवा गरज दर्शवणारी Helping Verbs.

---

# List of Modal Verbs

```text
Can
Could
May
Might
Shall
Should
Will
Would
Must
Ought To
```

---

# Important Rule

After every Modal Verb, use V1 (Base Form).

### Formula

```text
Subject + Modal Verb + V1
```

### Examples

✅ I can swim.

✅ She should study.

✅ We must complete the task.

---

## Common Mistake

❌ He can works.

✅ He can work.

---

❌ She should goes.

✅ She should go.

---

# 1. CAN

## Meaning

Used for:

- Ability
- Permission
- Informal Request

### Hindi

सकता हूँ / सकती हूँ

### Marathi

शकतो / शकते

---

## Ability

### Examples

- I can speak Marathi.
- He can drive a car.
- We can solve the issue.

---

## Permission

- You can enter.
- You can leave now.

---

## Request

- Can you help me?
- Can you check the logs?

---

## Office Examples

- I can join the meeting.
- We can deploy today.

---

## DevOps Examples

- I can troubleshoot Linux issues.
- We can restart the server.

---

# 2. COULD

## Meaning

Used for:

- Past Ability
- Polite Request
- Possibility

### Hindi

सकता था / कृपया

### Marathi

शकत होतो / कृपया

---

## Past Ability

- I could swim when I was ten.
- He could solve complex issues.

---

## Polite Request

- Could you help me?
- Could you please check this?

---

## Possibility

- It could rain today.
- The issue could be related to DNS.

---

## Office Examples

- Could you join the call?
- Could you review the document?

---

# CAN vs COULD

| Can             | Could        |
| --------------- | ------------ |
| Informal        | Polite       |
| Present Ability | Past Ability |
| Direct Request  | Soft Request |

---

### Examples

- Can you help me?
- Could you help me?

(Both are correct, but "Could" is more professional.)

---

# 3. MAY

## Meaning

Used for:

- Permission
- Possibility

### Hindi

सकता है / अनुमति

### Marathi

परवानगी / शक्यता

---

## Permission

- May I come in?
- May I ask a question?

---

## Possibility

- It may rain today.
- The release may be delayed.

---

## Office Examples

- May I join the meeting?
- The server may require maintenance.

---

# 4. MIGHT

## Meaning

Used for weak possibility.

### Hindi

शायद

### Marathi

कदाचित

---

## Examples

- It might rain.
- The application might fail.
- We might need additional resources.

---

## Office Examples

- We might postpone the deployment.
- The issue might be network-related.

---

# MAY vs MIGHT

| May                  | Might              |
| -------------------- | ------------------ |
| Stronger Possibility | Weaker Possibility |

---

### Example

- It may rain. (More likely)
- It might rain. (Less likely)

---

# 5. SHALL

## Meaning

Used for:

- Suggestions
- Offers
- Formal Future

---

## Examples

- Shall we start?
- Shall I help you?
- Shall we deploy today?

---

## Office Examples

- Shall we schedule a meeting?
- Shall we proceed?

---

# 6. SHOULD

## Meaning

Used for:

- Advice
- Recommendation

### Hindi

चाहिए

### Marathi

पाहिजे

---

## Examples

- You should learn English.
- We should complete testing first.
- He should exercise daily.

---

## Office Examples

- We should review the code.
- You should update the documentation.

---

## DevOps Examples

- We should create backups.
- We should monitor server performance.

---

# Common Mistake

❌ You should to study.

✅ You should study.

---

# 7. WILL

## Meaning

Used for:

- Future Actions
- Promises
- Decisions

### Hindi

करूँगा / होगा

### Marathi

करणार

---

## Examples

- I will call you.
- We will deploy tomorrow.
- She will attend the meeting.

---

## Office Examples

- I will send the report.
- We will investigate the issue.

---

# 8. WOULD

## Meaning

Used for:

- Polite Requests
- Preferences
- Imaginary Situations

---

## Polite Request

- Would you help me?
- Would you like some tea?

---

## Preference

- I would like to learn English.
- I would like to join the meeting.

---

## Office Examples

- Would you please review this?
- I would like to discuss the issue.

---

# WILL vs WOULD

| Will   | Would                       |
| ------ | --------------------------- |
| Direct | Polite                      |
| Future | Preference / Polite Request |

---

### Examples

- I will help you.
- I would like to help you.

---

# 9. MUST

## Meaning

Used for:

- Strong Necessity
- Obligation

### Hindi

ज़रूर / अवश्य

### Marathi

अवश्य / आवश्यक

---

## Examples

- You must wear a helmet.
- We must complete the deployment.
- Passwords must be secure.

---

## Office Examples

- We must fix the issue.
- The report must be submitted today.

---

## DevOps Examples

- Backups must be taken regularly.
- Security patches must be applied.

---

# MUST vs SHOULD

| Must      | Should      |
| --------- | ----------- |
| Necessary | Recommended |
| Strong    | Soft        |

---

### Examples

- You must submit the report.
- You should submit the report.

---

# 10. OUGHT TO

## Meaning

Used for moral duty or strong advice.

### Hindi

चाहिए

### Marathi

पाहिजे

---

## Examples

- You ought to respect your parents.
- We ought to follow best practices.

---

## Office Examples

- We ought to document the process.
- Engineers ought to review changes.

---

## Formula

```text
Modal Verb + Subject + V1 ?
```

### Examples

- Can you help me?
- Could you check this?
- May I come in?
- Should we deploy today?
- Will you attend the meeting?

# Common English Mistakes

❌ He can works.

✅ He can work.

---

❌ You should to learn English.

✅ You should learn English.

---

❌ We must to complete the task.

✅ We must complete the task.

---

❌ I would likes tea.

✅ I would like tea.

## English

1. I can speak English.
2. Could you help me?
3. We should deploy today.
4. We ought to take backups.

# Practice Exercise

Choose the correct Modal Verb.

1. You \_\_\_ learn English daily. (should/might)
2. \_\_\_ you help me? (Can/May)
3. We \_\_\_ complete the deployment today. (must/could)
4. It \_\_\_ rain tomorrow. (might/must)
5. I \_\_\_ like to join the meeting. (would/can)

---

# Answers

1. should
2. Can
3. must
4. might
5. would

# Key Points

✅ Modal Verb + V1

✅ Never use "to" after most modal verbs

✅ Use "Could" and "Would" in professional communication

✅ Use "Should" for advice

✅ Use "Must" for mandatory actions

✅ Modal Verbs make your English sound natural and professional

✅ Extremely useful for Meetings, Emails, Interviews, and Office Communication.
