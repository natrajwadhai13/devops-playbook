# Vocabulary

## What is Vocabulary?

Vocabulary means the collection of words that we know and use in speaking, writing, reading, and listening.

The more words you know, the easier it becomes to:

- Speak English
- Write Emails
- Attend Meetings
- Give Interviews
- Communicate in Office

---

## Hindi Meaning

Vocabulary का मतलब है शब्दों का संग्रह (Collection of Words)।

---

## Marathi Meaning

Vocabulary म्हणजे शब्दसंग्रह.

---

# Why is Vocabulary Important?

Grammar helps you make correct sentences.

Vocabulary gives you words to build those sentences.

---

## Example

Without Vocabulary:

❌ I do work.

With Vocabulary:

✅ I troubleshoot Linux servers.

✅ I deploy applications.

✅ I monitor infrastructure.

# Key Points

✅ Vocabulary = Collection of Words

✅ Grammar tells you HOW to speak.

✅ Vocabulary tells you WHAT to speak.

✅ Learn words in categories.

✅ Learn Meaning + Sentence Together.

✅ Use new words daily.

✅ Office English requires strong vocabulary.

✅ Vocabulary growth improves:

- Speaking
- Writing
- Interviews
- Emails
- Meetings
- Confidence

✅ One new word used correctly is better than ten words memorized.
