**Q1. Tell Me About Yourself**

Hello, my name is **Natraj Wadhai**.

I completed my **B.Tech and M.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering**.

I have **7+ years of IT experience**, including **Data Center Operations, Linux Administration, and DevOps**.

Currently, I am working as a **DevOps Engineer at Infosys**, supporting the **UBS client**. My current project involves migrating **20+ applications from Credit Suisse to UBS**.

I have worked in an **enterprise DevOps environment** where I have used and supported technologies such as **Linux, Docker, Kubernetes, GitLab CI/CD, AWS, Azure, AutoSys, ServiceNow, Grafana (LGTM), Ansible, Splunk, AppDynamics, and BigPanda** as part of **application migration, deployment, infrastructure provisioning, monitoring, and production support**.

My day-to-day responsibilities include environment provisioning, deployment support, troubleshooting infrastructure and application issues, monitoring production environments, coordinating change requests (RFCs), and working closely with cross-functional teams to ensure successful application migrations.

I also collaborate with global teams across **Switzerland, Zurich, the US, and India**, which has strengthened my technical, communication, and problem-solving skills.

I am now looking for an opportunity where I can further enhance my hands-on DevOps and Cloud expertise, take on more challenging responsibilities, and contribute to the organization's success while continuing to grow professionally.
