## üî¥ **TRAP 1: Scalability vs Elasticity**

**Question:**

C. High availability

‚úÖ **Correct Answer:** **B. Elasticity**

**Why?**

* **Scalability** = ability to scale, often manual or planned

‚ùå Many candidates choose **Scalability** ‚Üí TRAP

## üî¥ **TRAP 2: Availability Set vs Availability Zone**

**Question:**

C. Resource Group
D. Geo-replication

‚úÖ **Correct Answer:** **B. Availability Zone**

**Why?**

* Availability Zones = separate physical datacenters
* Availability Sets = racks & power (not full datacenter isolation)
